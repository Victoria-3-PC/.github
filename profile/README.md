## About Victoria 3

Victoria 3 is a grand strategy simulation game focused on nation-building, economics, diplomacy, and political management during the 19th and early 20th centuries. Players take control of a country and guide it through industrialization, social reforms, and global influence.

The game emphasizes deep economic systems, population management, and complex political structures. Every decision affects the development of society, from trade and production to laws and international relations.

With its detailed simulation mechanics, strategic depth, and historical setting, Victoria 3 delivers an immersive experience for players who enjoy complex management and long-term planning.
